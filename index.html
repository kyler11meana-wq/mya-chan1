About the Series

Sasaki To Miyano is a heartwarming story about the friendship and romance between two high school boys, Miyano and Sasaki. The series explores their journey of self-discovery, acceptance, and the challenges they face as their relationship deepens. Fans love its gentle humor, relatable characters, and emotional moments.
