Manga

Sasaki To Miyano is a manga series written and illustrated by Shou Harusono. It was first serialized online before being published in print. The story centers on Miyano, a shy boy who loves BL (Boys' Love) manga, and Sasaki, his outgoing upperclassman. As Sasaki becomes interested in Miyano's hobby, their friendship slowly blossoms into something deeper.

The manga is known for its gentle humor, relatable characters, and realistic depiction of LGBTQ+ themes. It has several volumes, each exploring different aspects of their relationship and the challenges they face. The manga explores themes of self-discovery, acceptance, and romance.

Anime

The anime adaptation of Sasaki To Miyano was produced by Studio Deen and aired in 2022. The series faithfully adapts the manga, focusing on the emotional growth of both main characters. The anime features beautiful animation, expressive character designs, and a memorable soundtrack.

The voice cast includes Yusuke Shirai as Sasaki and Soma Saito as Miyano, whose performances have been praised by fans. The anime covers key story arcs from the manga and includes special ending themes for each episode. It is available for streaming on several platforms and has received positive reviews for its heartfelt storytelling.
