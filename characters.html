Main Characters

Yoshikazu Miyano

Miyano is a shy, kind-hearted high school student who loves BL manga. He is thoughtful, sensitive, and sometimes insecure about his interests. His journey is about self-acceptance and opening up to others.

Shuumei Sasaki

Sasaki is Miyano’s upperclassman—outgoing, caring, and a bit mischievous. He becomes interested in Miyano’s hobby and gradually develops deeper feelings for him, supporting Miyano through his struggles.

Masato Hanzawa

Hanzawa is the reliable and friendly president of the disciplinary committee. He often gives advice to Miyano and Sasaki, and is known for his calm demeanor and ability to mediate conflicts among students.

Tasuku Kuresawa

Kuresawa is Miyano’s close friend and classmate. He is supportive and open-minded, always encouraging Miyano to be himself. Kuresawa is also in a happy relationship with his girlfriend, which he sometimes discusses with Miyano.

Taiga Haruno

Haruno is a cheerful and energetic member of the disciplinary committee. He enjoys teasing his friends and brings a lively atmosphere to the group, but is also caring and loyal.

Gonsaburo Tashiro

Tashiro is another member of the disciplinary committee. He is straightforward and honest, sometimes blunt, but always means well. He helps keep order at school and looks out for his friends.
